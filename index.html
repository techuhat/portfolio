About Me

Hey, I'm Mohammad Umar Khan – a developer, a digital thinker, and someone who's constantly exploring new ways to turn imagination into reality.

I like to build things that aren't just functional, but meaningful. Whether it's a portfolio, a blog, a cool new web app, or a futuristic UI – I try to make things that feel like me. Tech, for me, is more than just code. It's my way of expressing ideas, solving problems, and sometimes… just creating beauty out of logic.
